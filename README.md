## Add-Ons: Where the Bill Can Grow

A few things cost extra beyond the base subscription:

- **Residential proxies**: $3/GB — high-quality IPs for sites that block data center IPs
- **CAPTCHA solving**: $1–$1.50 per thousand — AI-powered bypass
- **Pay-per-result templates**: $0.001–$3 per thousand results — for heavily protected sites
- **Crawler setup service**: From $399 — Octoparse's team builds your scraper for you
- **Data service**: From $599 — they scrape and deliver the data, you do nothing

If you're scraping well-behaved sites on a Standard plan, you probably won't need any of these. If you're hitting protected e-commerce sites or social platforms, the proxy and CAPTCHA costs can add up fast.
